# Research Methodology

## Objective
Identify how top B2B SaaS operators use content for growth, trust, and revenue.

---

## Expert Selection Criteria
- Active practitioners (not just theorists)
- Proven results (audience, revenue, influence)
- Consistent content output
- Focus on B2B SaaS or related domains

---

## Content Collection Process

### LinkedIn
- Collected 3–5 recent posts per expert
- Focused on high-signal posts (frameworks, insights, strategy)
- Avoided low-value or generic posts

### YouTube
- Selected 1 relevant video per expert
- Prioritized strategy, growth, or content-related topics
- Extracted key insights and supporting evidence

---

## Filtering Criteria
- Relevance to B2B SaaS content strategy
- Actionable insights over generic advice
- Evidence of real-world application

---

## Limitations
- Some experts had limited YouTube presence
- In such cases, high-quality interviews or podcasts were used

---

## Outcome
A structured dataset of content insights across 10 experts, enabling pattern recognition and strategy development.

# Ross Simmonds — YouTube Analysis

## Video
[Watch Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kdSNhN6Wcc4)

## Summary
- “Create once, distribute forever” philosophy
- Focus on maximizing content ROI
- Strong emphasis on repurposing

## Key Insights
- Distribution > creation in modern content
- Repurposing increases reach exponentially
- Content should exist across multiple formats

## Framework
- Start with one idea
- Convert into multiple formats (text, video, carousel)
- Distribute across channels
- Repeat

## Connection to LinkedIn Strategy
- LinkedIn content should be repurposed from core ideas
- Supports scalable content systems

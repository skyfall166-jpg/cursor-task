## Topic

## Key Insights
### 1. Content must align with offers
- Insight: Content should directly connect to monetization strategy  
- Evidence: Hormozi emphasizes that content without a revenue path is ineffective  
- Example: “Content without monetization is wasted effort”  
- Why it matters: Prevents creating content that gets engagement but no business results  

### 2. Value-first content builds authority fast
- Insight: High-value, actionable content accelerates trust  
- Evidence: Repeated focus on giving away valuable insights upfront  
- Example: Breaking down real business frameworks openly  
- Why it matters: Positions creator as expert and attracts high-intent audience  

---

## Summary
- Focus on monetization-driven content strategy  
- Content should directly support business goals  
- Emphasis on value-first approach

1. Create value-heavy content  
2. Align content with offers

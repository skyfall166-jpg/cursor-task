## Key Insights

### 1. Content success comes from continuous experimentation
- **Insight:** Content performance improves through testing formats, timing, and frequency  
- **Evidence:** Emphasis on treating LinkedIn as a testing environment rather than a fixed strategy  
- **Example:** Trying different formats (video, text, posting cadence) and analyzing results  
- **Why it matters:** Reduces guesswork and helps identify what actually drives engagement and pipeline  

### 2. Audience understanding drives organic growth
- **Insight:** Organic content only works when deeply aligned with audience needs  
- **Evidence:** Repeated focus on audience-first thinking over creator preferences  
- **Example:** Creating content based on buyer pain points rather than trends  
- **Why it matters:** Increases relevance, engagement, and conversion potential  

### 3. Community > broadcasting
- Building relationships and engagement matters more than one-way posting  

---
## Framework
1. Test multiple formats (video, text, frequency)  
2. Analyze performance  
3. Double down on what works  
4. Add paid distribution to scale  

- Content → pipeline (indirect revenue impact)
